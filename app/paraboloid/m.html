The dynamic paraboloid model of magnetosphere was developed in the Laboratory of planet magnetospheres of the Institute of Nuclear Physics, Moscow State University. This model enables calculations of changes in magnetic field of large-scale msgnetospheric current systems using observational data obtained during calcm and disturbed periods of solar activity. The model can be used to forcast cosmic weather, to study the influence of cosmic factors on the human wealth, to protect space craft from radiation as well as in the applied fields of space environment physics.
