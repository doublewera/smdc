INTERHELIO-Zond PROJECT
[General information] [Instrument] [Participants] [RU]

"INTERSONG" device allows to detect:

  • neutrons ( 0.01 - 5 MeV, 20 - 100 MeV )
  • x-ray radiation ( 0.03 - 0.1 MeV )
  • gamma - rays of the energy range from 0.1 MeV to 20 MeV to cover lines from major constituents of solar atmosphere

"SKI-5" device allows to detect:

  • protons ( 1.6 - 24 MeV, 80 - 200 MeV )
  • electrons ( 0,04 - 2,5 MeV )
  • ions, including alpha - particles, ( from 2.5 MeV / nucl to 120 MeV / nucl )

The instrumental suite consists of three blocks:

  • "INTERSONG" was developed to detect neutral radiation produced during a solar flare and serves for registration of neutrons, x-ray and gamma - ray radiation
  • The block of detecting of a charged particles "SKI-5-D" - serves for registration of the electrons, protons and ions
  • The electronics unit "SKI-5-E"- serves to collect the data, their storage and for a transmission of the data into telemetry system
